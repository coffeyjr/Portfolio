ETSU Parking

This is a project I tackled during my last semester of college in the spring of 2023. I wanted to create something that ETSU students, faculty, and staff could use to improve the way they parked on campus.

One of the pages includes an interactive map of campus, that I built using JavaScript, with various parking lots outlined, numbered, and color-coded for the right person. Since I didn't have access to the data of the parking lots, the numbers that are generated for how many spots are remaining are randomized.

The other page includes various links to forms, applications, forms, etc. as well as the parking and traffic regulations brochure.

I built this website using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript.
